We provide the perfect service for you.

India is an agricultural country where in about 70% of the population depends on agriculture. Farmers have wide range of diversity to select suitable Fruit and Vegetable crops. A large section of their income is generated by horticulture and vegetable farming. Agricultural production mainly depends on the quality of seed, soil etc. In order to obtain more valuable products, a product quality control is basically mandatory. The quality of agricultural products may be reduced due to leaf diseases.The classical approach for detection and identification of leaf diseases is based on the naked eye observation by the experts. In some developing countries, consulting experts are expensive and time consuming due to the distant locations of their availability. Automatic detection of leaf diseases is essential to automatically detect the symptoms of diseases as early as they appear on the growing fruits.

Our applications provides one such approach where the farmers could easily upload the images of the leaves of the plants and our application will help them to diagnose the disease in the plant. Further they can also browse to respective disease page to know more about its counter and preventive measures.
